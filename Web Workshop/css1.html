Cricket Legends

Here are some of the most celebrated cricketers in the world.

About VK18

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in New Delhi, India, is a renowned international cricketer known for his right-handed batting prowess. He has served as the captain of the Indian national team across all formats and is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in cricket history.

RS45

Rohit Sharma, born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, India, is a distinguished right-handed batsman and the current captain of the Indian cricket team in limited-overs formats. Renowned for his elegant stroke play and ability to anchor innings, Sharma has etched his name among cricket's elite..

KLR01

Kannaur Lokesh Rahul, commonly known as KL Rahul, is an Indian international cricketer born on April 18, 1992, in Bangalore, Karnataka. A right-handed wicket-keeper batter, he represents Karnataka in domestic cricket and has played for various franchises in the Indian Premier League (IPL). .

MSD07

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer renowned for his exceptional leadership and wicket-keeping skills. Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, India, Dhoni captained the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. Under his captaincy, India achieved significant milestones, including victories in the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2011 Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy, making him the only captain to win all three major ICC trophies..
